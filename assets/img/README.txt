Recommended: square crop, 600×600 px or larger, JPG/PNG.

You can also place other images here (e.g., for blog posts) and reference them as
assets/img/your-image.jpg from index.html / cv.html, or ../assets/img/your-image.jpg
from inside the blog/ folder.
